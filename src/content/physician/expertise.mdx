### Specialized Foot & Ankle Care

Dr. Nikki Lam specializes in comprehensive diagnosis and treatment for complex foot and ankle conditions using both conservative and surgical approaches.

- Achilles tendon injuries
- Chronic heel pain
- Plantar fasciitis
- Ankle instability
- Arthritis of the foot and ankle
- Sports-related injuries

#### Advanced Treatment Options

- Minimally invasive surgery
- Ultrasound-guided injections
- Regenerative medicine therapies
- Custom orthotic solutions
